Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Quiz Answer Assistant ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 License Key and Authentication Data

  • License Key: We collect and store your license key to authenticate and authorize access to the Extension
  • Device Fingerprint: A unique device identifier (SHA-256 hash) is generated to prevent license sharing
  • Device Information: Browser type, platform, and basic system information
  • Storage: License keys are stored locally in your browser using Chrome/Firefox storage API
  • Transmission: License keys are transmitted to our backend server (extension-kiek.onrender.com) for verification

1.2 Email Address

  • Collected during purchase for license recovery purposes
  • Stored in our database associated with your license key
  • Used only for license retrieval and account management
  • Not shared with third parties

1.3 Usage Data

  • Last verification timestamp (to manage offline grace period)
  • Last used date (for license activity tracking)
  • IP address (logged for security purposes)
  • Activation count (to detect suspicious activity)

1.4 Questions and Answers

  • Selected question text is sent to our backend server for processing
  • Our server forwards questions to DeepSeek AI API for answer generation
  • We do NOT store or log the questions you submit
  • We do NOT store or log the answers provided
  • Questions are processed in real-time and immediately discarded

1.5 Payment Information

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay
  • We do NOT collect or store credit card information
  • We receive only payment confirmation and order IDs from Razorpay
  • Email address is collected before payment for license delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your license key and grant access to the Extension
  • Service Delivery: To process questions and provide AI-generated answers
  • Security: To prevent unauthorized access, license abuse, and fraud
  • Device Binding: To enforce one-license-per-device policy
  • License Recovery: To allow users to retrieve lost license keys via email
  • Support: To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • License keys stored locally in browser using encrypted storage API
  • Device fingerprint stored locally
  • Last verification timestamp cached locally

3.2 Server Storage

  • License data stored in MongoDB Atlas (cloud database)
  • Database hosted in secure data centers with encryption at rest
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only

3.3 Security Measures

  • All communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • API keys and secrets stored in environment variables
  • Device fingerprints use SHA-256 cryptographic hashing
  • Payment processing secured by Razorpay (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 DeepSeek AI

  • Purpose: AI-powered answer generation
  • Data Shared: Question text only (no personal information)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.deepseek.com/privacy-policy
  • Note: Questions are sent via our backend server, not directly from your browser

4.2 Razorpay (Payment Processing)

  • Purpose: Secure payment processing
  • Data Shared: Payment information, email address
  • Privacy Policy: https://razorpay.com/privacy/
  • Compliance: PCI DSS Level 1 certified

4.3 Render.com (Hosting)

  • Purpose: Backend server hosting
  • Data Stored: License database, server logs
  • Privacy Policy: https://render.com/privacy
  • Location: United States data centers

4.4 MongoDB Atlas

  • Purpose: Database hosting
  • Data Stored: License information, user emails
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Security: Encryption at rest and in transit

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party services listed above to operate our Extension
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Retention

  • Active Licenses: Retained as long as license is active
  • Expired Licenses: Retained for 90 days after expiry, then deleted
  • Deactivated Licenses: Retained for 30 days, then deleted
  • Questions/Answers: NOT stored (processed in real-time only)
  • Server Logs: Retained for 30 days for security purposes
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • View License: Check your license status in the extension popup
  • Retrieve License: Use the "Forgot License" feature with your email
  • Delete Data: Logout from the extension to clear local data
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension to stop all data collection

7.2 Privacy Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are in the EU or California, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

7.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email provided in the Contact section below.
