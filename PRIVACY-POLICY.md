# Privacy Policy for Quiz Answer Assistant

**Last Updated:** March 9, 2026

## Introduction

Quiz Answer Assistant ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### License Key
- We collect and store your license key to authenticate and authorize access to the Extension
- The license key is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- The license key is transmitted to our backend server (extension-kiek.onrender.com) for verification

### Usage Data
- We log when your license key is used to verify authentication
- We record the last time your license was verified
- No personal browsing history or website content is collected

### Questions and Answers
- Selected question text is sent to DeepSeek API (api.deepseek.com) for processing
- We do not store or log the questions you submit
- Answers are displayed temporarily and not saved

## How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication:** To verify your license key and grant access to the Extension
- **Service Delivery:** To process questions and provide answers via AI technology
- **Security:** To prevent unauthorized access and license abuse

## Data Storage and Security

- License keys are stored locally in your browser
- License verification data is stored in our MongoDB database
- All communications with our backend use HTTPS encryption
- We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties

## Third-Party Services

### DeepSeek API
- Question text is sent to DeepSeek API for answer generation
- DeepSeek's privacy policy applies to data processed by their service
- Visit: https://www.deepseek.com/privacy-policy

### Render (Hosting)
- Our backend is hosted on Render.com
- Render's privacy policy applies to server infrastructure
- Visit: https://render.com/privacy

## Data Retention

- License keys are retained as long as you remain logged in
- You can delete your data by logging out of the Extension
- License verification logs are retained for security and abuse prevention

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your license key information
- Request deletion of your license key
- Opt-out by uninstalling the Extension

## Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
